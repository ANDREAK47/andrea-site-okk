About the Project

Every year, thousands of orphaned teenagers struggle to find stable and supportive foster homes. At Hope for Teens, our mission is to bridge the gap between teenagers in need and families willing to open their hearts.

Our Mission

We aim to create a future where every teenager has a safe and loving home. Our approach includes:

  • Identifying teenagers in need of foster care.
  • Recruiting and screening potential foster families.
  • Providing support, counseling, and training for both teens and families.
  • Working with local authorities to facilitate placements.
